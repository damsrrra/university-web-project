The Four Pillars
Goal Definition Examples
Eco Protection Helps maintain biodiversity, clean air, and water. Establishing marine protected areas, reducing deforestation, promoting renewable energy.
Ocean Waste Harms marine life, enters the food chain, damages ecosystems. Recycling programs, plastic bans, ocean clean-up initiatives like The Ocean Cleanup.
Marine Protection Creating marine reserves, reducing overfishing, controlling coastal development. The Great Barrier Reef Marine Park, Palau's National Marine Sanctuary.
Sustainable Fisheries Quotas, seasonal bans, eco-friendly fishing gear, aquaculture. Marine Stewardship Council (MSC), Aquaculture Stewardship Council (ASC).

Blue Horizon promotes ocean conservation and sustainability through education, advocacy, and community engagement.
